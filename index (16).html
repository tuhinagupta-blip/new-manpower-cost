Calculation Methodology

YTD Actual
(CTC÷365)×days from max(1 Apr, DOJ) to min(today, LWD).
Projected FY
(CTC÷365)×eligible days. Notice: till LWD. Active: till 31 Mar.
New HC Prorated
(Annual CTC÷365)×days from start date to 31 Mar. Open positions only.
Landing Cost
Inc-adj current + prorated new HC. Updates live with increment inputs.
